Step 1: Research

Strategic Planning

Begin by defining the target audience and their objectives. This initial phase involves aligning research goals with user needs to ensure the project's success.

Scope & Framework

Once audience requirements are established, it's crucial to delineate the scope of the research and its structural framework. This includes determining feasible research features and understanding the interconnectedness of research elements.

Step 2: DATA COLLECTION

Framework Development

Proceed to develop the research framework, outlining the methodology, data collection techniques, and analysis strategies. This stage involves refining the research design to effectively address research objectives.

Visualization

Visualize research findings using appropriate tools and techniques. Whether through charts, graphs, or interactive visualizations, conveying data in a comprehensible format is key to enhancing understanding and engagement.

Step 3: ANALYSIS & INTERPRETATION

Data Analysis

Conduct thorough analysis of collected data, employing statistical methods and analytical tools to derive meaningful insights. This phase involves identifying patterns, trends, and relationships within the data.

Interpretation

Interpret research findings within the context of existing literature and theoretical frameworks. By critically evaluating results and drawing conclusions, the research contributes to advancing knowledge in the field.
